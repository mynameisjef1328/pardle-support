Pardle Privacy Policy

Simple, transparent, and no creepy surprises.

Support Privacy

Your privacy

Pardle is built to be fun and lightweight. This policy explains what information is collected, why it is collected, and the choices you have.

Effective date:
App: Pardle

Information Pardle may collect

Pardle may collect the following categories of information, depending on the features you use:

  • Game data you enter, such as player names (if you type them), hole scores, notes, selected course, and game codes.
  • App functionality data, such as a device-generated identifier used to keep shared games synced and prevent abuse.
  • Diagnostics, such as crash logs and performance data, to improve stability.

What Pardle does not collect

  • No request for your contacts, photos, microphone, or precise location.
  • No selling of your personal information.
  • No third-party advertising profiles.

How your information is used

  • To save and sync games across devices and players (multiplayer).
  • To calculate totals and stats relative to par.
  • To diagnose bugs, fix crashes, and improve performance.
  • To respond to support requests if you contact us.

Third-party services

Pardle may use Firebase (Google) services such as Firebase Authentication (including anonymous sign-in), Cloud Firestore for game syncing, and crash or performance diagnostics. These services process information on our behalf to operate the app and keep it reliable.

If you are using a build that includes analytics, Pardle may also collect basic usage events to understand what features are working and what needs improvement.

Sharing

Pardle shares information only as needed to provide the app’s core features (example: syncing a shared game), operate infrastructure (example: Firebase), comply with legal obligations, or protect the security of the service.

Data retention

Game data is retained for as long as needed to provide the service. If you want a game deleted, contact support and include the game code. We will make a reasonable effort to remove it, unless we need to keep it for legal or security reasons.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards designed to protect your information. No method of storage is 100% secure, but we aim to keep things locked down and minimal.

Children’s privacy

Pardle is not designed to collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal information through the app, contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

Your choices

  • You can avoid typing personal info into player name fields or notes.
  • You can request deletion of a game by contacting support with the game code.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us at help@pardle.app.
